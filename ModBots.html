Mod Bots

May 2021 - Aug 2021
Overview

Mod Bots is an arena combat game where you and your friends fight off waves of enemies and fearsome gladiators! Claim the weapons of your fallen enemies and use them to show the world your might!


Mod Bots was a game that I worked on with a group of my classmates in order to keep up our skills heading into senior year. Our aim was to have a product that we could publish on a platform such as Steam. This was my first time working on a networked game, as well as working on a project that was meant to be shipped and distributed. As always there were ups and downs throughout the development process, but working on Mod Bots provided a valueable learning experience for me, and boosted my confidence heading into my final year at college.


Check out the game here on Steam!
