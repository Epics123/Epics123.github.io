Audio Visualizer

Made in Unreal Engine 4 | Dec 2020

This is a fun little project that I decided to take on over Christmas break. I had recentely been getting back into using Unreal Engine and I thought that making an audio visualizer would be a great way to re-familiarize myself with the engine. I'm very happy with what I was able to achieve, and have admittedly spent a large amount of time switching out songs and watching how the visualizer reacted. I was able to acieve the result you see above using a combination of C++ and Unreal's Blueprint visual scripting language.


Song used: Battle! (Champion) - Jun'ichi Masuda
