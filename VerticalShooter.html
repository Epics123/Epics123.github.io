Vertical Shooter

Top Down Vertical Shooter Made in Unity | Feb 2019

This is a simple yet fun 2D vertical shooter I made in Unity! Shoot through waves of aliens and try to get the highest score you can! Don't forget to grab powerups along the way to give you the edge in battle!

This was the first time I had to work on a game by myself and have my peers review and play it. I only had about two weeks to create something that was playable, had a loss state, and resembled a vertical shooter. Programmer art aside, I'm very proud of what I was able to accomplish. There are an endless amount of enemy waves that can spawn that each get progressivly harder than the last. The player can also pick up an item that will change their weapon type for a few seconds. I was even able to experiment with particle systems for the background and explosions.
