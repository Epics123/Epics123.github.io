Dev Blog #1: Where are you as a developer?

2/12/2021

Over the last two years, I feel like I have grown significantly as a game developer. After taking various different classes, I feel much more confident in my skills as a programmer across a variety of topics including graphics programming, AI, and gameplay programming. I have been able to branch out and explore different aspects of programming in class, as well as tacking personal projects to broaden my horizons even more. In one of the recent prototypes I worked on for my Production II class, I was able to tackle implementing an Echolocation effect (seen on the left), which I had never done before. It is still very much a work in progress, but I am very happy with what I was able to accomplish in one week.

After completeing Prodution I, I feel like I was really able to develop soft skills like communication, giving/recieving feedback or critique, and the ability to problem solve in team situations. I feel comfortable working with people in different disciplines, and confident that I can explain my discipline effectively to others. I really enjoy working as part of a team, and I thrive off of being able to bounce my ideas off of other people.

Over this next semester, I want to continue to broaden my horizons with programming, as there is always something new to learn about. I also want to continue to improve my current skillset th ensure that I can deliver the highest quality work possible. In order to achieve this, I will continue to research areas of programming that are new to me. Try them out for myself or collaborate with friends. There is still so much more for me to learn and I can't wait to continue on this journey!
