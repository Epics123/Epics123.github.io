Solarsnap (Greenlight Prototype)

Feb 12, 2021 - Feb 26, 2021

What is Solarsnap? You play as a lone fire mage living in a remote snowy tundra. You were heading home one night and a blizzard caught you off guard. Through the thick snow cover you are unable to see the sinkhole ahead of you and are thrown from your sled and into the darkness below. Make your way through the ruins of the past and get home before you freeze. You must be careful though, your abilities drain your body heat and there is only so much warmth to go around.


Solarsnap is a game that I worked on in my Game Production II/Game Studio II class. The trailer you see above is from our greenlight presentation, which is where we present our game to 80+ people including our peers and professors. If greenlit, my team will absorb new members and continue to work on the game for the rest of the Spring 2021 semester. Currently, my team is till waiting to be approved for production, but we are extremely proud at what we were able to accomplish in a very short and rigorous time frame.
