Hi! I'm Ethan Heil, a Fourth Year Game Programming Major at Champlain College. I am an extremely motivated student who is always looking for ways to learn something new. I have a background in Java programming, and have developed skills in C++, C#, GLSL, and version control tools such as Git during my time at Champlain.

Through my studies I have been able to to foster important soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and the ability to adapt to a situation. I have worked with multiple cross-disciplinary teams, and have presented the team's work to professors and fellow peers. Additionally, I have been able to gain experience working with industry tools including game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine 4. I am very interested in various aspects of graphics and animation programming, though I also enjoy gameplay and UI programming as well.

I'm a huge gamer at heart, but I often find myself stopping to think about how a feature or mechanic was implemented. I love knowing the intricacies of how things work, and that is a big motivator for me to learn as much as I can. Plus, there are just so many cool things that can be done through programming!
